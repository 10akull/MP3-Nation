ABOUT

"I Fall Apart" is a song by American rapper Post Malone from his album Stoney (2016). The song was released on October 17, 2017 by Republic Records as the sixth single, after a viral performance on social media.


BACKGROUND

"I Fall Apart" was included on Post Malone's 2016 album, Stoney, and never originally released as a single, but a live performance of the song on September 24 went viral. Video of the performance was posted by a fan on Twitter, and has earned more than 200,000 retweets. It was later uploaded on Facebook, and has tallied more than 7.9 million global views.

CHART PERFORMANCE

The song has peaked at number 16 on the Billboard Hot 100 as well as peaking at number 19 on the UK Singles Chart, making it Post Malone's highest charting single from Stoney in the United Kingdom.
